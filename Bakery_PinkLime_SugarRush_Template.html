Our Story

Started with a
kitchen & a dream

Sugar Rush started in 2012 when Maria & Carlos Torres turned their Brooklyn apartment kitchen into a cupcake operation. Word spread fast. Lines formed. The rest is sweet history.

Today we bake over 300 items daily from our East Village shop, using the same recipes that started it all: real butter, local eggs, no shortcuts. Every cake is still hand-decorated. Every cookie is baked with love and a little bit of chaos.
