Our Story

From Abuela's kitchen to your table

When Rosa Fernandez opened a tiny bakery on Calle Ocho in 1998, she had one mission: bring the flavors of her Cuban childhood to the heart of Miami. Every morning, she was up at 3 AM, kneading dough and filling the neighborhood with the scent of fresh bread.

Today, her granddaughter carries on that legacy. Same recipes. Same passion. Same love in every loaf, every pastelito, every cake. Three generations of baking, one family tradition.
