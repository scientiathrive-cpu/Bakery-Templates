Our Story

Three Generations of Baking Love

What started as Abuela's kitchen recipes in 1998 has grown into the neighborhood's favorite bakery. We still use the same family recipes, the same dedication, and the same love in every single item we bake.

Every morning at 4 AM, our ovens fire up to bring you the freshest breads, pastries, and cakes in the city. We source local ingredients whenever possible and never use preservatives.
