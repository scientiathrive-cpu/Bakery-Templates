🌷 Our Story

Baked with heart in Austin, TX

Bloom Bakehouse was founded in 2018 by pastry chef Maya Chen after a decade of working in Parisian patisseries and Tokyo cafés. Her mission: bring world-class pastry to Austin with a playful, colorful, Texas-sized personality.

Every item is handmade daily using organic flour, local dairy, and seasonal fruits. We don't do artificial colors — our pastels come from beet powder, butterfly pea flower, matcha, and turmeric. Real ingredients, real magic.
